9-Days Tour Package

  • Day 1-2: Sigiriya and Dambulla

Travel to the ancient city of Sigiriya, home to the famous Sigiriya Rock Fortress, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Also, explore the stunning Dambulla Cave Temples and the vibrant local markets.

  • Day 3: Kandy

Travel to the cultural city of Kandy, home to the famous Temple of the Tooth Relic, which is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Also, explore the stunning Royal Botanic Gardens, the scenic Kandy Lake, and the vibrant local markets.

  • Day 4: Nuwara Eliya

Travel to Nuwara Eliya, known as "Little England" due to its colonial-style architecture and temperate climate. Take a scenic train ride from Kandy to Nuwara Eliya and visit the tea plantations, Gregory Lake, and the stunning Lovers Leap waterfall.

  • Day 5: Yala National park

Yala National Park is the most visited national park in Sri Lanka and is renowned for its wildlife, particularly its population of leopards. The park is also home to elephants, buffalo, crocodiles, and a variety of bird species. Visitors can take a safari tour of the park, which offers a chance to see the animals up close and in their natural habitat.

  • Day 6-7: Mirissa

Mirissa is a charming coastal town located in the southern part of Sri Lanka. Known for its stunning beaches, crystal clear waters, and vibrant marine life, Mirissa is a popular destination for tourists from all over the world. Whether you're looking to relax on the beach, go surfing or snorkeling, or simply enjoy the local cuisine and culture, Mirissa has something for everyone. With its laid-back atmosphere, friendly locals, and breathtaking natural beauty, it's no wonder that Mirissa is considered one of Sri Lanka's top travel destination

  • Day 8: Colombo

Visit the historic Galle Face Green, Independence Square, and the National Museum. You can also indulge in some shopping at the popular Pettah market and dine at some of the city's famous restaurants.
