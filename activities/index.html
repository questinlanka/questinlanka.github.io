Beaches (May 12, 2018)
Sri Lanka is home to some of the most stunning beaches in the world, with crystal-clear waters, pristine white sands, and breathtaking views. Whether you’re looking to soak up the sun, indulge in water sports, or simply relax and unwind, Sri Lanka has a beach that’s perfect for you. Here are some of the top beaches to visit in Sri Lanka. One of the most popular beaches in Sri Lanka is Unawatuna, located on the southern coast of the island. Read more
Cultural Activities (May 12, 2018)
Sri Lanka has a rich cultural heritage that spans thousands of years, and there are plenty of opportunities to explore and experience the country’s cultural traditions and activities. Here are some of the top cultural activities to check out in Sri Lanka: Visit ancient temples and ruins: Sri Lanka is home to numerous ancient temples and ruins, many of which are UNESCO World Heritage Sites. These sites offer a glimpse into the country’s rich history and cultural traditions, and are a must-visit for anyone interested in Sri Lanka’s past. Read more
Foods & Cuisine (May 12, 2018)
Sri Lankan cuisine is a fusion of different flavors and culinary traditions, influenced by the country’s history and diverse population. Here are some of the top foods and dishes to try in Sri Lanka: Rice and curry: Rice and curry is the staple dish of Sri Lanka, and consists of a plate of steamed rice served with a variety of curries, chutneys, and pickles. The curries can be vegetarian or meat-based, and are typically flavored with spices such as cumin, coriander, and turmeric. Read more
Hiking & Trecking (May 12, 2018)
Adam’s Peak: Adam’s Peak is a sacred mountain located in the central highlands of Sri Lanka, and is a popular destination for pilgrims and hikers alike. The mountain is known for its stunning views and the famous “Sri Pada” or “Sacred Footprint” at the peak. The trek to the top of the mountain is challenging, with over 5,000 steps to climb, but the views and spiritual experience make it well worth the effort. Read more
