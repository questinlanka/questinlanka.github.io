Historycle Sites (May 12, 2018)
Sri Lanka is a land of rich history and culture, with a plethora of ancient sites that reflect the country’s rich past. From ancient Buddhist temples to stunning fortresses, Sri Lanka has a lot to offer for history enthusiasts. One of the most popular historical sites in Sri Lanka is the Sigiriya Rock Fortress, also known as the Lion Rock. Built in the 5th century by King Kashyapa, this towering fortress stands at an impressive 660 feet and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Read more

Sri Lanka is famous for its tea plantations, which produce some of the finest teas in the world. The island’s tea industry dates back to the 19th century, and today, Sri Lanka is one of the world’s largest exporters of tea. Here are some of the top tea plantations to visit in Sri Lanka.
